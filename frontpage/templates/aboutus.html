About Dr.Auto

Dr.Auto is the perfect two-wheeler servicing & repairing app in India that offers end customers two-wheeler service or repair at their doorstep at competitive prices.

Dr.Auto is a Gurgaon-based automobile technology company that facilitates doorstep two-wheeler service and repair through a combination of our asset-light bike service app and an easy-to-reach customer helpline number +91 8001-96-8001. We offer a 21st-century mobile app experience for real-time two-wheeler service, repair & roadside assistance and are among the leading platforms for doorstep bike services in India. We enable end-customers to maintain their two-wheelers conveniently anywhere anytime at a predictable cost. It is the only bike servicing app providing i nstant real-time booking of a bike mechanic at your doorstep within a guaranteed time limit of half an hour. Instead of wasting your precious time in a dingy mechanic shop, utilize it with your loved ones and book Dr.Auto to tackle your bike problem.

We are covering all major two-wheeler brands, including electric vehicle brands, across 10 tier-1 cities in India. We plan to expand in every part of the country to cater to the large portion of two-wheeler riders.

We are entirely bootstrapped and backed by a hardworking , dedicated & fun loving team.
